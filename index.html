Bio

Garrett is a junior on the Skyline XC team. After a mediocre freshman year and a season-ending injury during his sophomore year, Garrett was more determined than ever to run well. He was one of the most consistent runners on the team, earning a top 7 spot in every race.

He excelled not only in running, but also in motivating others by leading cheers, keeping everyone hyped up, and staying positive. His favorite memories were from camp, where the team spent time together playing games and staying full of energy. His favorite race was at Holly, when the team placed first and went on stage together with a big trophy.
